COMPANY OVERVIEW

Sri Kajang Mining Sdn Bhd (SKMSB) is a Malaysian young and dynamic mining company, focused on tin & gold mining, exploration and production based in Terengganu Malaysia. SKMSB was established in 2020 with the sole purpose of servicing the tin mining and gold mining industry. Our key focus areas include among others; contract mining & other mining operations services.

We promote formation of sustainable partnership and relations with our clients, consultant, relevant authority, vendors and local community.
